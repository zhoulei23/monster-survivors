Monster Survivors

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival game that puts you in the middle of a monster-infested world. Your mission is to survive against waves of terrifying creatures while gathering resources and upgrading your abilities.

How to Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Click or tap to attack monsters
  3. Collect resources and power-ups
  4. Upgrade your abilities between waves
  5. Survive as long as possible!
